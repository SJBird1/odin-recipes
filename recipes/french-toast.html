French Toast

Damn good french toast

Follow this receipe to become a french toast expert

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk (probably)
  • 1/4 tsp of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl. Add the milk and cinnamnon. Beat until well mixed.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Soak a slice of bread in the egg mixture and then place in the pan. Cook for 3 minutes on each side.
  4. Repeat step 3 for the remaining pieces of bread.
  5. Serve with maple syrup and berries, if desired
