Egg McMuffin

Make an egg McMuffin at home

Is McDonald's too far? This recipe teaches you how to make a McMuffin at home

Ingredients

  • 1 english muffin
  • 1 slice of ham
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook your egg over hard in a small skillet
  2. Meanwhile, cook you piece of ham in another skillet
  3. When your egg and ham are almost done, cut your english muffin in half and toast in the toaster
  4. Butter the english muffin halves. Place the cheese slice, followed by the egg and then the ham onto the bottom muffin. Put the top on.
  5. Enjoy
