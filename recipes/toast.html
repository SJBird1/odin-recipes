Toast

This method will get you the best toast ever

Have you ever wondered how to make the pefect piece of toast? Look no further; the recipe will teach you how to do just that.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • A bit of butter

Instructions

  1. Set your toaster setting 4 or 5
  2. Place the slices of bread into the toaster and turn the toaster on
  3. In a few minutes the toast will pop up. Take the toast out and place it on a plate
  4. Butter each piece of toast
